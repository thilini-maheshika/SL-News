97,000 Heroin addicts in Sri Lanka:
National Dangerous Drugs Control Board

The Government of Sri Lanka has decided to establish a new rehabilitation centre for drug addicts. According to statistics at the National Dangerous Drugs Control Board, there are around 97,000 people who are addicted to Heroin across the island. The data show that close to 20,000 people are addicted to the narcotic substance ICE and around 400,000 are addicted to Cannabis.
